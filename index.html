C3F™ Framework

A proprietary diagnostic for non-financial risks in founder-led acquisitions. Four dimensions: Culture, CEO, Control, Founder. Three engagement formats: pre-closing diagnostic, 100-day stabilization, and selective board mandates.

Financial due diligence protects entry valuation. C3F™ protects post-closing value creation.

Unlike a management assessment, C3F™ maps the risk of friction post-closing between stakeholders and whether the governance architecture will either contain or amplify that friction.
